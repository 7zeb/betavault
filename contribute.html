Why Contribute?

BetaVault thrives on community contributions. By sharing your beta builds, you help preserve software history and make it accessible to everyone.

How to Contribute

Follow these steps to contribute your beta builds:

  1. Prepare your beta build files (e.g., ISO, VHD, or other formats).
  2. Ensure the files are properly labeled with version and build numbers.
  3. Upload your files to a file-sharing service (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox).
  4. Send us the download link using the form below or via email.
